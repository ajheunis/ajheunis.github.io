Who I Am

Technology and Innovation

I'm a seasoned executive with a deep-rooted passion for technology, particularly in the realms of software and data.

My journey began in the picturesque Boesmansriviermond, a small village nestled along the stunning Eastern Cape coastline of South Africa. This beautiful, rural backdrop of my childhood sparked a contrasting fascination in technology that has stayed with me throughout my life.

The Beginning

After completing my studies, I embarked on my professional journey by developing a computerized quality system for my father's manufacturing business. This project not only honed my technical skills but also instilled in me a profound appreciation for practical, real-world applications of technology.
